# Ratings and Reviews

> The best way to earn positive ratings is to deliver a great experience; equally important is choosing the right moment to ask.

**Platforms:** iOS, iPadOS, macOS, tvOS, visionOS, watchOS

## Best Practices

Use the system-provided prompt on iOS, iPadOS, and macOS.

- **Ask only after demonstrated engagement** — not on first launch, not during onboarding. Good prompting moments: completion of a game level, task, or meaningful milestone.
- **Don't interrupt tasks** — find natural breaks or stopping points.
- **Don't pester.** Allow ≥1–2 weeks between requests; prompt again only after additional meaningful engagement.
- **Use the system-provided prompt.** The system checks for previous feedback and — if none — displays a non-intrusive in-app rating/review request. People can dismiss with one tap; they can opt out system-wide. The system automatically limits display to **3 times per app per 365 days**.
- **Weigh the trade-off before resetting your summary rating.** Resetting reflects the current version but reduces total ratings, which can discourage downloads.
