# Designing for tvOS

> Apple TV delivers vibrant, immersive experiences for media, games, fitness, education, and home utility on the big screen.

**Platform:** tvOS

## Device Characteristics

| | |
|---|---|
| **Display** | Very large, high-resolution TV |
| **Ergonomics** | Stationary viewing from ~8+ ft away; people may move around the room while interacting |
| **Inputs** | Siri Remote, game controllers, Siri, companion apps on other devices |
| **Usage pattern** | Deep single-experience immersion (often hours); picture-in-picture for parallel apps/video |

**Key system integrations:** TV app integration, SharePlay, Top Shelf, TV provider accounts.

## Best Practices

- Support fluid, familiar Siri Remote gestures — the remote is the primary interaction model.
- Embrace the focus system — it gently highlights and expands focused items, orienting people at all times. Never fight it.
- Deliver edge-to-edge artwork, subtle fluid animations, and engaging audio — cinematic, legible, captivating from across the room.
- Enhance multi-user support: make sign-in easy and infrequent, support shared sign-in, and auto-switch profiles when the current viewer changes.
