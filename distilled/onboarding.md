# Onboarding

> Help people get a quick start — fast, fun, and optional.

**Platforms:** iOS, iPadOS, macOS, tvOS, visionOS, watchOS

Onboarding occurs after launching completes. Ideally your app is intuitive enough to not need it, but when necessary, keep it minimal.

## Best Practices

- **Teach through interactivity** — let people actually do the task they're learning, not just read about it. Hands-on beats instructional.
- **Prefer context-specific tips** over a single upfront onboarding flow — surface tips at the moment they're relevant. Display instructions near the UI area they describe.
- **If you need a prerequisite flow:** keep it brief and entertaining. Never require memorizing large amounts of information.
- **Make tutorials optional** — let people skip on first launch. Don't re-present on subsequent launches. Make it easy to find later (help, settings, or account area).
- **Focus on your experience only** — don't teach people how the OS or device works.

## Additional Content

- **Splash screens:** design a beautiful, succinct graphic. Display just long enough to absorb at a glance — avoid the feeling that it's delaying the experience.
- **Don't let downloads block onboarding** — bundle enough content for immediate interaction; download the rest in the background.
- **Avoid displaying legal/licensing in onboarding** — let the App Store handle agreements before download. If you must include them, integrate unobtrusively.

## Additional Requests

- **Defer nonessential setup and customization** — provide sensible defaults so people can start immediately.
- **Permission requests:** if your app requires private data access before functioning, integrate the request into the onboarding flow with a clear explanation of why it's needed. Otherwise, request permission at the moment the specific feature is first accessed.
- **Ratings and purchases:** let people experience the app before prompting. People respond more positively after genuine engagement.

## Platform Considerations

*No additional considerations for any platform.*
