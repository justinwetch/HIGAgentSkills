# Rating Indicators

> A rating indicator uses horizontally arranged symbols (stars by default) to communicate a ranking level.

**Platforms:** macOS only

Displays only complete symbols (rounds values). Symbol spacing is fixed; doesn't expand or shrink to fit width.

## Best Practices

- **Allow inline rank adjustment** — in lists of ranked items, let people adjust rankings without navigating to a separate screen.
- **Custom symbols must have clear purpose** — stars are universally recognized as ranking symbols; alternative symbols may not communicate a rating scale.
